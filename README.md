> Kitchen Chaos is a 3D single and multiplayer game developed using the Unity Game Engine. In the game, you take on the role of a chicken chef who must prepare and complete incoming food orders within a limited time to earn money. While the core concept is a simple cooking game, Kitchen Chaos stands out with enhanced mechanics, responsive controls, and lively animations that make the gameplay more engaging and chaotic.
